Fansipan - The Roof of Indochina

Introduction

Fansipan is the highest mountain in Vietnam, standing at 3,147.3 meters above sea level. It is famous for its breathtaking landscapes, sea of clouds, and rich biodiversity.

Facts about FANSIPAN

  • The sunrise and sea of clouds on Fansipan are among the most beautiful views in Northwest Vietnam.
  • The weather changes quickly; in a single day, it can be sunny, cloudy, windy, and foggy.
  • At 3,147.3 meters, Fansipan is the highest mountain in Vietnam and the Indochina Peninsula.
